## Citation Style Guide

### APA Style (Recommended)
**In-text**: (Author, Year) or (Author1, Author2, & Author3, Year)
**Reference**: Author, A. A., Author, B. B., & Author, C. C. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Journal, Volume*(Issue), pages. DOI

### Multiple Authors
- 1-2 authors: (Smith & Jones, 2020)
- 3-5 authors: (Smith, Jones, & Brown, 2020) - first citation
- 6+ authors: (Smith et al., 2020)

### Multiple Citations
- Same author, different years: (Smith, 2018, 2020)
- Different authors: (Smith, 2020; Jones, 2019)

### Direct Quotes
Include page number: (Smith, 2020, p. 45)

### Secondary Sources
"as cited in" format: (Johnson, 2015, as cited in Smith, 2020)
