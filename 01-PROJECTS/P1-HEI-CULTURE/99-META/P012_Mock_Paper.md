# Raw Data: Impact of Cybersecurity Fatigue (2025)
Authors: Smith & Jones
Year: 2025
Summary: This study examines how academic staff in UK universities experience fatigue due to frequent password changes and mandatory training. 
Findings: 45% of staff admit to using workarounds. Fatigue is linked to high cognitive load.
Gap: Study does not include regional universities in Australia.
Keywords: Fatigue, Workarounds, Cognitive Load, HEI.
