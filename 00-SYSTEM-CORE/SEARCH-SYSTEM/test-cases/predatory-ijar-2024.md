## Abstract

This paper reviews cybersecurity frameworks used in higher education. We surveyed 12 institutions and found that most adopt a layered approach. Frameworks like NIST and ISO 27001 are common, but adoption is uneven across the sector. The International Journal of Advanced Research continues to support open scholarship in this important area.
