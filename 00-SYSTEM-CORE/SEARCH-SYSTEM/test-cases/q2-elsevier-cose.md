## Abstract

This Q1 paper studies cybersecurity behaviour in higher education institutions across 12 universities. Mixed-methods design with 500 survey participants and 30 interviews. Policy compliance emerged as the strongest predictor. Findings inform ISO 27001 implementation guidance for the higher education sector.
